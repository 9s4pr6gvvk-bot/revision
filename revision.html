📋 Paper 3 Case Study Research — Biscuits & Confectionery
Key Companies & Market Context
Market leaders: McVitie's (pladis), Mondelez (Cadbury, Oreo), Nestlé (KitKat), Mars, Ferrero. UK biscuit market ~£3bn. Confectionery ~£4.5bn.

Key themes to revise for P3: Ansoff Matrix (product development — reduced sugar lines), Porter's Five Forces (supermarket own-label threat), financial ratio analysis (ROCE for capital-intensive manufacturing), HR (shift workers, automation vs employment), global strategy (export markets, MNCs sourcing cocoa ethically).

Current issues: Cocoa price surge 2024 (commodity risk), sugar tax implications, sustainability/palm oil pressure, premiumisation trend vs cost-of-living squeeze on discretionary spend.

Essay angle: "To what extent is innovation the most important factor in maintaining competitive advantage in the confectionery industry?" — evaluate with: financial strength, brand equity, pricing strategy, and global distribution.
