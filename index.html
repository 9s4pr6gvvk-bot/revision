📋 Paper 3 Case Study Research — Biscuits & Confectionery Industry
Key Companies, Market Context & Essay Angles
Market leaders: McVitie's (pladis), Mondelez (Cadbury, Oreo), Nestlé (KitKat), Mars, Ferrero. UK biscuit market ~£3bn annually. Confectionery ~£4.5bn.

Key themes for Paper 3: Ansoff Matrix (product development — reduced sugar lines), Porter's Five Forces (supermarket own-label threat), financial ratio analysis (ROCE for capital-intensive manufacturing), HR strategy (automation vs employment), global strategy (MNCs sourcing cocoa ethically, Fairtrade supply chains).

Current issues: Cocoa price surge 2024-25 (commodity risk management), sugar tax implications on pricing strategy, sustainability/palm oil ESG pressure, premiumisation trend vs cost-of-living squeeze on consumer discretionary spend.

Model essay angle: "To what extent is innovation the most important factor in maintaining competitive advantage in the confectionery industry?" — evaluate with financial strength, brand equity, pricing strategy, and global distribution.
