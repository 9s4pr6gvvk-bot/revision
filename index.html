📋 Paper 3 Case Study — Biscuits & Confectionery Industry
Key Companies, Market Context & Essay Angles
Market leaders: McVitie's (pladis), Mondelez (Cadbury, Oreo), Nestlé (KitKat), Mars, Ferrero. UK biscuit market ~£3bn. Confectionery ~£4.5bn.

Key themes: Ansoff Matrix (product development — reduced sugar lines), Porter's Five Forces (supermarket own-label threat), ROCE for capital-intensive manufacturing, MNCs sourcing cocoa ethically via Fairtrade supply chains.

Current issues: Cocoa price surge 2024-25 (commodity risk), sugar tax implications, sustainability/palm oil ESG pressure, premiumisation vs cost-of-living squeeze.

Model essay angle: "To what extent is innovation the most important factor in maintaining competitive advantage in the confectionery industry?" — evaluate with financial strength, brand equity, pricing strategy, global distribution.
